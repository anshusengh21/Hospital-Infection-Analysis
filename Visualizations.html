Visualizations

The Hospital Infection Analysis is done by Comparing 4 common Mortality reasons that are:

  • Road Accident
  • HIV
  • Breast Cancer
  • Hospital Infection

The case study is of few Countries like Australia, Austria, Belgium, Brazil, Canada, Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece,
Hungary, Iceland, Ireland, Israel, Italy, Japan, Korea, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Mexico, Netherland, New Zealand, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Russia, Slovak Republic, Slovenia,
South Africa, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Turkey, United Kingdom and United States. Total 41 Countries mortality data of the 4 death causes are included.
The data which has been used includes total of 17 years of data that is from year 2000 to 2016.

For data visualization Tableau tool has been used.

As we are considering 41 Countries and 17 years of data. The total death count for all the countries due to above 4 reasons form 2000 till 2016 is 15806690.

This is a Tableau Dashboard for the visualization purpose. We have made up 5 graphs for visualizing the problem of Hospital Associated Infection. The graphs include 5 different categories and has the variations among them. They are as follows:

  • Mortality Percentage
  • More Mortality Due To Hospital Infection
  • Gender wise Mortality Rate
  • Country wise Total Death Count
  • Top3 & Bottom3 Countries
